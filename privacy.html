Privacy statement

Data collection

Four pieces of data is automatically collected:

  • Time of HTTP request
  • Your public IP-address
  • Your User-Agent
  • What page was requested
These are all provided by your browser as part of a standard HTTP request.
I do not collect or process personally identifiable information.

Data storage

Data collected is stored in the form of plain-text log files on a private server located in Sweden.
It is regularily backed up to external storage devices.

Data retention

Data collected is retained indefinetly unless manually deleted.

Data usage

Data collected is used strictly in one of five ways:

  1. To gauge the number of unique visitors over time.
  2. To understand what pages, if any, are popular among visitors.
  3. To understand whether page contents are more popular in certain countries or not.
  4. To understand whether desktop or mobile devices are more popular among visitors.
  5. To block IP-addresses from further HTTP-requests if they exhibit malicious behaviour.
I have no commercial interest in collecting data.

Cookies

No Cookies are used on this website.

Third party access

No third parties have access to the data collected.

External resources and assets

Resources and assets needed to render any given page on this website, such as fonts and images, are directly provided by the website. As such, your browser does not need to fetch resources or assets from a third-party, such as a Content Delivery Network, to render page content.

Contact

If you have questions with regards to this privacy statement, don't hesitate to contact me using one of the methods outlined on the Contact page.
